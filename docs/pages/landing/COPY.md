## SECTION 8 — FAQ

**Headline:**
`Questions.`

*(Short, direct, confident — not "Frequently asked questions")*

**Q1:**
- Q: `Is it really free?`
- A: `Yes. Free forever. No credit card. Your page exists on the free tier as long as you want it to. No time limit, no trial expiry.`

**Q2:**
- Q: `What happens to my Linktree?`
- A: `Paste your Linktree URL in onboarding and we import your links in one step. Keep Linktree if you want — or delete it. Your call.`

**Q3:**
- Q: `Do I need a Spotify page?`
- A: `No. ABLE works with SoundCloud, Bandcamp, YouTube, or nothing at all. If you don't have any streaming links yet, your page still works.`

**Q4:**
- Q: `Who owns my fans' emails?`
- A: `You do. We cannot contact your fans. Export your full list at any time, even on the free tier. The relationship is yours.`

**Q5:**
- Q: `How is this different from Linktree?`
- A: `Your page shifts with what you're doing. Linktree is a static list of links. ABLE has pre-release mode, live mode, gig mode — and it switches automatically based on your release date.`

**Q6:**
- Q: `What if I'm not releasing anything right now?`
- A: `Your evergreen profile is beautiful on its own. Most artists use the Profile state 90% of the time. The campaign states are there when you need them.`

**Q7:**
- Q: `Can I cancel?`
- A: `Yes, any time. No notice required. Your page stays live on the free tier.`
