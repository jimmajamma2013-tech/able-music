## What "10/10" means here

The strategy document scores 10/10 as a specification. The execution scores 10/10 when:
- Every platform in the audit table has a confirmed, current status
- Every P0/P1 handle is secured and in 1Password
- Every active account has a completed profile
- Every dormant account is branded and has a redirect post pinned
- The universal handle is decided and recorded
- The content calendar is live (see `SOCIAL-MEDIA-PLAN.md` for that layer)
