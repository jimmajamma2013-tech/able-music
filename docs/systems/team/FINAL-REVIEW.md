## Decisions this document makes explicit

1. **Hire order is fixed**: Community Manager → Engineer → Marketing. Not interchangeable.
2. **Artist calls stay with James until a playbook exists**: this is a product protection decision.
3. **No agency hires for first 5**: direct sourcing only. Signal about genuine interest.
4. **Paid trial tasks are non-negotiable**: fairness and quality signal in one step.
5. **48-hour decision rule**: respects candidates and forces clarity.
6. **Health is infrastructure**: flexible working is policy, not exception.

## Risks this document acknowledges

- The Community Manager hire may need to happen before £2k MRR if artist call volume grows faster than expected. That's fine — the trigger is "blocking growth", not the exact MRR number.
- The engineer salary (£55–70k) is a stretch at £5k MRR. A part-time or fractional engineer may be the bridge. This is worth flagging.
- Marketing hire at £8k MRR assumes healthy margins. If Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) is high, this timeline shifts.
