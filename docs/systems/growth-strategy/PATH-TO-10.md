## What keeps the system from 10/10

**Gap 1: The Abler programme has no existing Ablers**
The programme is fully designed. The commission structure is right. The outreach sequence is in `GROWTH_STRATEGY.md`. The gap is execution — the first 20 producer contacts haven't been made yet. This section becomes 10/10 when the first 5 Ablers are enrolled and producing referrals.

**Gap 2: No Colombia operational plan**
The strategic rationale for Colombia is strong. What doesn't exist is the operational plan: who is the community hire candidate, which Medellín music collectives are the entry points, what is the Spanish localisation timeline. These belong in a dedicated `docs/systems/growth-strategy/COLOMBIA-EXPANSION.md` file when the time comes.

**Gap 3: The metrics dashboard is a spec, not a live tool**
The 8 metrics are defined. The kill signals are defined. The three-state interpretation framework is defined. What doesn't exist is the actual dashboard — the platform admin view where James can see all 8 metrics on one screen. This is a product build dependency, not a doc dependency. Track it in `docs/STATUS.md`.

**Gap 4: The "one channel" has not been identified yet**
The framework for finding it is correct. But the finding happens through 12 weeks of testing. The document will be 10/10 when the one channel is named, not just described as a framework.
