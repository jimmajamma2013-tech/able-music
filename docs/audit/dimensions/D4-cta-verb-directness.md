# Dimension D4 — CTA Verb Directness
**Category:** Copy, Voice & Messaging
**Phase:** 4

Every action button, link, and interactive element label across all pages must use a direct, specific verb. Banned CTA labels: "Go", "Continue" (minimum; specific is better), "Submit", "OK", "Yes/No" (without context), "Click here", "Learn more", "Sign up" (as a fan CTA), "Next". This audit checks every button on every page against these rules, then assesses whether the chosen verb is the best available — not just acceptable but optimal.
