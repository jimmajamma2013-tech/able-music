# Dimension J1 — Email Deliverability
**Category:** Launch Readiness & Cross-Page Coherence
**Phase:** 10 (Launch)
**Status:** Not started

Resend domain verification complete. SPF, DKIM, and DMARC records set for `ablemusic.co`. Fan confirmation email tested end-to-end: correct artist name, accent colour, campaign-state copy, CTA link. Not landing in spam. This dimension is a hard launch blocker — no DNS record means every confirmation email goes to spam, destroying the trust of the first fans an artist signs up. Full compliance means every email variant (profile, pre-release, live, gig state) renders correctly in Gmail, Apple Mail, and Outlook, carries correct unsubscribe headers, and passes MX Toolbox DNS validation before any real artist goes live.
