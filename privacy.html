Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · ABLE (ablemusic.co)

Who we are

ABLE is a platform for independent musicians operated by ABLE Music Ltd (the data controller). You can contact us about data at privacy@ablemusic.co.

What this is

This policy explains what data we collect, why, the legal basis, and your rights. We keep it short because we mean it.

Data we collect

Fan sign-ups

When you sign up to stay close to an artist on ABLE, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time you signed up
  • The source of your visit (e.g. Instagram, TikTok, direct)
  • Your consent timestamp and version

Legal basis: Your explicit consent, given at the point of sign-up.

This data is held on behalf of the artist. The artist uses it to contact you directly about their music, shows, and releases. ABLE processes it as a data processor on their behalf. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything else.

Retention: Until you unsubscribe, or until the artist deletes their ABLE account, whichever comes first.

Artist accounts

When artists create an account we collect their name, email address, and profile information they choose to share.

Legal basis: Performance of contract (running the ABLE service).

Retention: For the duration of the account, plus 30 days after deletion to allow recovery.

Analytics

We collect anonymised data about how pages are used — page views, CTA clicks, traffic sources. This data is used to show artists how their page is performing. It is not linked to individual fans.

Legal basis: Legitimate interest (product analytics to improve the service).

Retention: Aggregated analytics are retained for 24 months, then deleted.

Your rights

Under UK/EU data protection law you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of data we hold about you
  • Rectification — correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data
  • Restriction — ask us to pause processing while a dispute is resolved
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Object — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — unsubscribe from an artist's list at any time using the link in any email. Withdrawal does not affect processing that happened before withdrawal.

To exercise any of these rights, email privacy@ablemusic.co. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK supervisory authority: Information Commissioner's Office (ICO), Wycliffe House, Water Lane, Wilmslow, SK9 5AF.

Cookies and local storage

ABLE uses browser local storage and cookies for analytics (PostHog, EU-hosted) and session management. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking pixels.

Data storage

Data is stored securely via Supabase (EU region) and Netlify. We do not transfer data outside the UK/EU without appropriate safeguards (standard contractual clauses where applicable).

Sub-processors

We use the following third-party processors to operate the service:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (EU region, Dublin). Privacy policy
  • Netlify — static hosting and serverless functions (EU region available). Privacy policy
  • PostHog — product analytics (EU-hosted, anonymised data only). Privacy policy
  • Microsoft Clarity — session replay for UX research (anonymised, no PII recorded). Privacy policy

We keep this list current. Each processor is bound by a data processing agreement and may not use your data for their own purposes.

Children's data

ABLE is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13. If you believe a child has signed up, contact privacy@ablemusic.co and we will delete the data promptly.

Data breach notification

If we become aware of a breach that is likely to result in risk to your rights or freedoms, we will notify the ICO within 72 hours and affected individuals without undue delay, in line with UK GDPR Article 33-34.

Automated decision-making

We do not use your data for automated decision-making or profiling that produces legal or similarly significant effects.

Contact

Questions about privacy: privacy@ablemusic.co
