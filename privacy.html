Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · ABLE (ablemusic.co)

What this is

ABLE is a platform for independent musicians. This policy explains what data we collect, why, and what we do with it. We keep it short because we mean it.

Data we collect

Fan sign-ups

When you sign up to stay close to an artist on ABLE, we collect:

  • Your email address
  • The date and time you signed up
  • The source of your visit (e.g. Instagram, TikTok, direct)

This data belongs to the artist, not to ABLE. The artist uses it to contact you directly about their music, shows, and releases. We do not sell it, share it with advertisers, or use it for anything else.

Artist accounts

When artists create an account we collect their name, email address, and profile information they choose to share. This is used to run their ABLE page and dashboard.

Analytics

We collect anonymised data about how pages are used — page views, CTA clicks, traffic sources. This data is used to show artists how their page is performing. It is not linked to individual fans.

Your rights

You can unsubscribe from an artist's list at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email they send you. To request deletion of your data, email privacy@ablemusic.co.

Cookies

ABLE uses minimal cookies — session storage only. We do not use third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels.

Data storage

Data is stored securely via Supabase (EU region) and Netlify. We do not transfer data outside the EU/UK without appropriate safeguards.

Contact

Questions about privacy: privacy@ablemusic.co
