Most relevant for ABLE: Music UX lineage

Tobias van Schneider (#09) — Designed Spotify's early desktop app visual language and Beats Music. The neon-on-dark aesthetic and editorial boldness he brought to Spotify is the most direct precedent for music streaming UI design. His writing on "taste" is required reading.

Wilson Miner (honorable mention) — Designed Rdio, widely considered the most beautifully designed music streaming product ever made. His "When We Build" talk (2011) is essential viewing for anyone designing for digital music. Rdio's UX set benchmarks Spotify later iterated toward.

Jason Yuan (see UI Designers) — Worked on Apple Music UI and created Mercury OS, a speculative OS rethink. His ambient color bloom effect (color radiating from album artwork) has been widely copied across music apps including Apple Music's current implementation.
